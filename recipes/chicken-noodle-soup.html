Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

A homestyle Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe

ingredients

  • chicken
  • Chicken Broth
  • Small pasta nodles
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • onions
  • garlic
  • butter

Steps

  1. Saute the carrots onion garlic and celery in the butter
  2. add the broth
  3. add the chicken
  4. cook noodles
  5. enjoy
